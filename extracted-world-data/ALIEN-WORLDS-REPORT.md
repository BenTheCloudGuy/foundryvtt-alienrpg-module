## 3. HEART OF DARKNESS — 26 DRACONIS SYSTEM DETAIL

**Source:** alienrpg-hod + alienrpg-starterset

### The System
- **Name:** 26 Draconis System
- **Location:** +14 Spinward, 0 Coreward
- **Status:** Restricted
- **Original composition:** Trinary — two orange giants + one red dwarf
- **Current state:** Red dwarf collapsed into the **Ablassen Black Hole** ~75 years ago
- **Two remaining stars:** being consumed by the singularity

### LV-1113
- **Classification:** Small habitable planetoid in planetary debris belt
- **History:** USCSS Cronus landed here in 2111. Science team explored ancient Engineer ruins, collected artifacts (ampules of Chemical A0-3959X.91-15 "black pathogen"). Crew infected — some became Stage II Abominations. Ship became a "haunted" derelict.
- **Current status:** Destroyed/broken up. A chunk persists as **Asteroid 1113B**.
- **The 26 Draconis Strain:** A mutagenic pathogen derived from black pathogen; transforms hosts into Proto-Xenomorphs and ultimately the "Perfected" (Fulfremmen).

### Erebos Station
- **Classification:** Resurrection Class Plasma Trawling Platform / Recharge Station / Prison
- **Affiliation:** Weyland-Yutani
- **Length:** 1100m
- **Population:** 12 Administrators, 12 Corrections Officers, 99 prisoners, 36 Synthetics, one cat
- **Decks:** A (Astrometric Observatory), B (Operations), C (Health & Wellness), D (Reception & Sciences), E (Residence), F (Correction Modules), G (Main Concourse), H (Factory Complex), I (Lower Tower "The Sticks"), J (Engineering "The Boiler Room")
- **Key feature:** Orbits the Ablassen Black Hole; station becomes infected by the Living Proto-Hive
- **Covert project:** "Ironfish" intelligence operation (dual comms towers)

### Key NPCs & Locations Referenced
- **Star NN3553** — Site of a solar flare incident in '83 that killed 6 scientists
- **Torin Prime** — Civil War site; several characters reference service there
- **Arcturus** — Star Teacher artifacts discovered here (pictogram translations)
- **"Perfected Space"** — Coordinates hundreds of parsecs spinward, overlapping Earth's lost colonies
- **Gateway Station** — Mentioned as a reference point

## 9. MAJOR FACTIONS & ORGANIZATIONS

Referenced across all modules:
- **Weyland-Yutani** — Dominant megacorporation
- **Three World Empire (3WE)** — UK + Japan + developing nations
- **United Americas (UA)** — North/Central/South America merger
